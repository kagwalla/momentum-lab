## Market Note (more useful than a longer candidate list would be)

A general Kenya-wide veterinary directory (petsasa.co.ke, checked
2026-07-08) lists clinics across Nairobi, Kiambu, and the Coast — **zero
Kisumu entries**. This isn't evidence Kisumu has no other vet clinics; it's
evidence that Kisumu vet clinics have weak digital discoverability as a
market. That's independently useful market intelligence: it suggests low
digital competition for whoever establishes visibility first, which
strengthens Aniworld's case as a lead (they're the most digitally visible
option in a weakly-covered market) while also meaning a second candidate
may need offline discovery (physical scouting, word of mouth, Kisumu
business directories) rather than web search alone.
